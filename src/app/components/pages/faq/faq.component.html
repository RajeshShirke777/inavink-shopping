Can we order from different sellers at a time?

We have limited the order to one Particular Shop at a time. If you wish to order from different Shops, you may complete the order and place another order with different Seller.

How soon will I receive the order?

The delivery of your order is done by the Seller itself, most of the Shops displayed in In-A-Vink (as per the locations) are within 3-4 Km radius from you. Also, the Shop Timings plays important role in delivery of your order. You many even choose to visit the shop and collect the order or contact the seller and ask to delivery at the earliest.

What all Payment options do I get?

As the Seller themselves delivers the order. The payment is received by the Sellers directly without any Commission, thus, Payments options differ from Seller to Seller. However, at the time of placing the order as Home Delivery, you will receive options as CASH ON DELVIERY, SCAN ON DELIVERY, and SWIPE ON DELIVERY.
